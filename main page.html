About the Project

The Vehicle-to-Vehicle (V2V) Emergency Communication System is an intelligent transportation solution designed to improve road safety by enabling direct communication between nearby vehicles. The system allows vehicles to exchange real-time information about accidents, emergency situations, road hazards, and traffic conditions. When an emergency occurs, a vehicle can instantly broadcast alerts to surrounding vehicles, helping drivers take preventive actions and reducing the risk of secondary accidents. The system aims to enhance driver awareness, improve emergency response time, and contribute to safer and smarter transportation networks.
